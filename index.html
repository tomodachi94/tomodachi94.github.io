Hey there! I’m Tomodachi94, a programmer, gamer, wiki editor, and amateur archivist. This website hosts random stuff I write and want to share with the world.

What do you do?

I am pretty active in open source; I maintain several packages for Nixpkgs, as well as a niche package manager and the Awesome list of that same niche.

I am also active on Wikimedia projects, most notably Wikidata. I am also somewhat active on the FTB Wiki, the premier source of information on modded Minecraft.

I occasionally help out with ArchiveTeam, especially when they do stuff involving my hobbies and interests.

What tech do you use?

I’m a NixOS user. I use Neovim as my day-to-day editor, and I use Vivaldi as my preferred browser. I use the i3 window manager. My configurations for most of these programs is made publically available on GitHub.

As far as programming languages, I use Python and Lua, and I’m trying to learn Rust.

How can I talk to you?

I’m on Mastodon, Matrix, and GitHub. On most websites I use the handle tomodachi94, so there’s a good chance you’re talking to me if someone is using that handle. (If you’re unsure, I usually provide links to my Mastodon and Matrix accounts to make things clearer.) I also have a PGP key.

Please don’t use the links above for bug reports or feature requests; file an issue in the respective GitHub project instead.
